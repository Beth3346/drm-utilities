ELR Passwords

Analyzes passwords for complexity. A password is considered strong if it uses a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. It should also be st least 8 characters.

ELR Passwords also ensures that passwords are not on a list of the most commonly used passwords.
